The Rogue Sentinel

Akshan

Raising an eyebrow in the face of danger,
Akshan fights evil with dashing charisma,
righteous vengeance, and a conspicuous lack of shirts.
He is highly skilled in the art of stealth combat,
able to evade the eyes of his enemies and reappear when
they least expect him. With a keen sense of justice
and a legendary death-reversing weapon, he rights
the wrongs of Runeterra's many scoundrels while
living by his own moral code: “Don't be an ass.”

From Akshan's perspective, fighting was never about saving the world;
all he wanted was to resurrect his dead mentor with the power of the fabled resurrection weapon,
the Absolver. However, with the pain and learnings of multiple failed attempts, Akshan realized that his mentor
would rather he join the Sentinels' cause and use the weapon for good. As he joined the Sentinels and aided their many fights,
he came to terms with his mentor's death and realized the good he was able to achieve. His pursuit of selflessness paid off in the end, as he used the Absolver to turn the tides of a critical fight. By killing Isolde, he ended up resurrecting Senna and Gwen,
thus weakening Viego and allowing for his eventual defeat.
