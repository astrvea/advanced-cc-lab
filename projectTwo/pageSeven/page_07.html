The Exile

Riven

Once a swordmaster in the warhosts of Noxus,
Riven is an expatriate in a land she previously
tried to conquer. She rose through the ranks
on the strength of her conviction and brutal
efficiency, and was rewarded with a legendary
runic blade and a warband of her own.
However, on the Ionian front, Riven's faith in her
homeland was tested and broken.

From Riven's perspective, fighting the Ruination was about obtaining freedom.
She spent much of her life wrongly imprisoned in a Noxus jail cell, where she devised an escape plan
but had no one to help. However, with the help of the Sentinels, she was finally able to escape; out of gratitude, she devoted herself to stopping the Ruination and joined forces with the people who saved her. Things quickly took a turn for the worse when the man who imprisoned her, Draven, became Ruined by the Black Mist and demanded them to fight for their freedom.
Resolved to keep her life, Riven fought once again and finally obtained true freedom.
