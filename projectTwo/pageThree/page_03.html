The Ruined King

Viego

Once ruler of a long-lost kingdom,
Viego perished over a thousand years ago
when his attempt to bring his wife back
from the dead triggered the magical catastrophe
known as the Ruination. Transformed into a powerful,
unliving wraith tortured by an obsessive longing
for his centuries-dead queen, Viego now stands as
the Ruined King, controlling the deadly Harrowings
as he scours Runeterra for anything that might one
day restore her, and destroying all in his path as
the Black Mist pours endlessly from his cruel, broken heart.

From Viego's perspective, everything is worth sacrificing
if it means bringing back his wife. This is a man who won't stop at anything
to not get what he wants, but needs. His beautiful wife, Isolde, was taken away early from him,
and Viego does not know how to stop his mission of vengance. He desperately searches all over the world for pieces of Isolde's soul to capture them and bring her back to life. One by one he searches, growing more obsessive and crazy as time goes on. He slowly grows unrecognizable, hurting and killing everyone in his way. Eventually, he is able to ressurect Isolde by collecting all of her souls. Her spirit is revived, but only for a few minutes. Through this limited time, Isolde is finally
able to speak to Viego directly. However, instead of showing her love and appreciation, she condemns him. She is gravely disappointed at what Viego has become, leaving Viego
stunned and heartbroken- a moment of weakness. This moment of weakness is
what ultimately leads to Viego's downfall, allowing the Sentinels of Light to capture him
and imprison him in the Shadow Isles forever. Still, Viego's perspective remains the same;
nothing would cause him to take back all the damage he created if it meant
seeing Isolde one last time.
