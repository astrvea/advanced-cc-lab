The Purifier

From Lucian's perspective, keeping Senna alive and safe after losing her once before was all he wanted.
His love and devotion to Senna mirrors but opposes Viego, as the Ruination is precisely what could take her away from him again. Though he was used to either working alone or as a pair with just Senna, he knew that Viego's threat was now too big to handle alone; thus, he set off to the old Sentinel of Lights Headquarters to find old wisdom and new allies. As he followed the Black Mist into many battles and new locations, Lucian stopped at nothing to ensure the safety of his love and his life.
