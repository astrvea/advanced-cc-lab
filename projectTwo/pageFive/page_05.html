Gwen

A former doll transformed and brought to life
by magic, Gwen wields the very tools that once created her. She carries the weight of her maker's love with every step, taking nothing for granted. At her command is the Hallowed Mist, an ancient and protective magic that has blessed Gwen's scissors, needles, and sewing thread.
So much is new to her, but Gwen remains joyfully determined to fight for the good that survives in a broken world.

The Hallowed Seamstress

From Gwen's perspective, she saw how deeply and beautifully Isolde loved
Viego while she was still alive. With Viego's wife, Isolde, as the seamstress who created Gwen, she tragically passed away before her creation came to life. As Viego's power and reign grew and grew, Gwen was brought to life. Taking upon her creator's tools, she knew that Viego had to be stopped. Gwen knew of Isolde's true feelings; Isolde wanted her love to mean something. She wanted her and Viego's love to be celebrated by all, not the cause of ruin all throughout the world. Gwen knew how bad Viego's
obsession had become and she made it her mission to make sure that no other life would be ruined by him. Although Gwen was from the Shadow Isles and created from Isolde herself, she stands with those hurt by Ruination. Seeing all the destruction firsthand, she promises that she will do all that she can to protect others from the chaos, with the life she was given by her maker's love.
