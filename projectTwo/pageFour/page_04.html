Thresh

Sadistic and cunning, Thresh is an
ambitious and restless spirit of the Shadow Isles.
Once the custodian of countless arcane secrets,
he was undone by a power greater than life or death, and
now sustains himself by tormenting and breaking others
with slow, excruciating inventiveness. His victims
suffer far beyond their brief mortal coil as Thresh wreaks
agony upon their souls, imprisoning them in his unholy
lantern to torture for all eternity.

The Chain Warden

From Viego's perspective, everything is worth sacrificing if it means
bringing back his wife. This is a man who won't stop at anything to not get what he wants,
but needs. His beautiful wife, Isolde, was taken away early from him, and Viego does not know how to stop his
mission of vengance. He desperately searches all over the world for pieces of Isolde's soul to capture them and bring her back to
life. One by one he searches, growing more obsessive and crazy as time goes on. He slowly grows unrecognizable, hurting and killing everyone
in his way. Eventually, he is able to ressurect Isolde by collecting all of her souls. Her spirit is revived, but only for a few minutes. Through this limited time, Isolde is finally able to speak to Viego directly. However, instead of showing her love and appreciation, she condemns him.
She is gravely disappointed at what Viego has become, leaving Viego stunned and heartbroken- a moment of weakness.
This moment of weakness is what ultimately leads to Viego's downfall, allowing the Sentinels of Light to capture
him and imprison him in the Shadow Isles forever. Still, Viego's perspective remains the same;
nothing would cause him to take back all the damage he created if it meant
seeing Isolde one last time.
